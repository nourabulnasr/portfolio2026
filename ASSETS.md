# Replacing portfolio assets

Replace these files in `src/assets/` with your own so the site shows your content:

## Project screenshots (1–5)

Replace these with your 5 website screenshots, **in order**:

| File            | Your screenshot |
|-----------------|-----------------|
| `project-1.png` | 1st (e.g. Elgabaly Architects) |
| `project-2.png` | 2nd (e.g. The Outcast EG) |
| `project-3.png` | 3rd (e.g. El Serafy) |
| `project-4.png` | 4th (e.g. Destinations) |
| `project-5.png` | 5th (e.g. Power of Sea Moss) |

You can overwrite the existing `.png` files or add new ones with these exact names.

## Hero portrait

Replace `hero-portrait.png` with your photo (the one with the dark jacket). It will be shown with a light blur in the hero section.

---

Your provided images are in Cursor’s assets folder. Copy them into `src/assets/` and name them as above.

## WhatsApp link

In `src/components/ContactSection.tsx`, the WhatsApp href is `https://wa.me/201234567890`. Replace **201234567890** with your full phone number **with country code and no + or spaces** (e.g. Egypt: `20` + your number). Example: `201234567890` for Egypt.
